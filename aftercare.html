TATTOO AFTERCARE

Follow these guidelines to ensure your tattoo heals properly and stays looking its best.

1

Leave The Bandage On

Keep the protective covering on for the amount of time recommended by your artist.

2

Wash Gently

Clean the tattoo using lukewarm water and mild, fragrance-free soap.

3

Moisturize

Apply a thin layer of the recommended aftercare cream as directed.

4

Avoid Scratching

Your tattoo may itch while healing. Do not pick or scratch the area.

5

Stay Out Of The Sun

Avoid direct sunlight while healing and always use sunscreen afterward.

6

No Swimming

Avoid pools, hot tubs, baths, and oceans until your tattoo has healed.

Healing Timeline

Days 1–3

Redness, tenderness, and slight swelling are normal.

Days 4–14

Peeling and itching may occur as the skin heals.

Weeks 2–4

Surface healing should be nearly complete.

Weeks 4–8

Deeper layers continue healing beneath the skin.

PIERCING AFTERCARE

Proper aftercare is essential for healthy healing and to prevent infection.

1

Clean Twice Daily

Use a sterile saline solution to gently clean your piercing morning and evening.

2

Do Not Touch

Avoid touching your piercing with unwashed hands.

3

Avoid Swimming

Stay away from pools, hot tubs, lakes, and oceans during healing.

4

Don't Change Jewelry Early

Wait until the piercing is fully healed before changing jewelry.

5

Sleep Carefully

Avoid putting pressure on fresh piercings while sleeping.

6

Watch For Infection

Persistent redness, swelling, heat, or discharge should be checked immediately.

AVERAGE PIERCING HEALING TIMES

Earlobe

6–8 weeks

Helix

6–12 months

Nostril

2–6 months

Navel

6–12 months

When To Contact Us

If you experience excessive swelling, unusual discharge, severe pain, or signs of infection, contact the studio immediately.
