MEET THE ARTIST

Lead Tattoo Artist

Siyabonga Mhlongo

With nearly a decade of experience in the tattoo industry, Siya has been creating custom body art since 2017. What began as a passion for body art developed into a career dedicated to helping clients express themselves through unique, meaningful tattoos.

Entirely self-taught, Siya has spent years refining techniques, developing artistic skills, and building a reputation for quality workmanship. Every tattoo is approached with creativity, precision, and attention to detail, ensuring that each client leaves with a piece they can be proud of for years to come.
